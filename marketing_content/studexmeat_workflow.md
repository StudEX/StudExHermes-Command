### 3️⃣ Boost & Amplify (Immediately after each upload)

1. **Add to Instagram bio** – temporary link: `www.studexmeat.com?src=insta`.
2. **Pin the TikTok video** to the profile for 24 h.
3. **Create a simple UTM‑tagged URL** for each CTA (e.g., `www.studexmeat.com?utm_source=tiktok&utm_medium=video&utm_campaign=wagyu_gold`).
4. **Schedule a Story/Highlight** on Instagram with the same video (use the 15 s “Swipe Up” sticker if you have the link).
5. **Cross‑post to Facebook Page** (use native repost to reach an older audience).
6. **Send a brief newsletter** (if you have an email list) with the four videos embedded and a “Shop Now” button.

---

### 4️⃣ Engagement & Community Management (Live)

- **Monitor comments** for the next 2 h after each post; reply with friendly, brand‑voice messages and include the link when needed.
- **Run a quick poll** in Instagram Stories: "Which video did you love most?" – drives algorithmic boost.
- **Add the relevant hashtags** as the first comment on Instagram to keep captions clean.
- **Use TikTok’s “Duet”** feature – encourage users to show their own bite of the biltong.

---

### 5️⃣ Analytics & Quick Wins (End‑of‑Day)

- Pull **TikTok & Instagram Insights** (views, likes, shares, click‑through rate to website).  
- Pull **YouTube Shorts** data (views, watch time, traffic source).  
- Record numbers in a simple spreadsheet (date, video, platform, impressions, clicks, CVR).  
- Identify the best‑performing video and **boost** it with $20‑$30 of paid promotion (target interests: "foodies", "gym", "military").  
- Update the website banner with the winning video thumbnail and a direct “Shop Now” button.
