## 1. Creating Our "AI Influencer" (The Consistent Face of StudEx)
*Reference: Higgsfield Popcorn & AI Character Consistency*

We don't need to hire expensive influencers if we build our own perfectly consistent brand ambassadors using **Higgsfield Popcorn**.

### The Workflow:
1. **The Anchor Identity:** Use the "Photorealistic Character Identity Sheet" prompt you developed earlier to generate the baseline images of our AI Influencer.
2. **Anchor in Popcorn:** Upload this identity sheet into Higgsfield Popcorn. Popcorn’s "Intelligent Visual Memory" will lock onto the facial structure, lighting, and style logic.
3. **Generate Scenes:** When you prompt for Day 5 ("Boxer ice bath + Biltong pack" or "Midnight training montage"), Popcorn will place *our exact AI character* into that environment without facial drift or style mismatch.
4. **Lipsync Studio:** To have the AI influencer speak directly to the camera (e.g., the Day 7 "The Invitation" video), move the Popcorn-generated image into **Higgsfield Lipsync Studio**, add the script, and generate the talking UGC ad.

## 2. Viral Product Ads in Minutes 
*Reference: Sora 2 Trends for Viral Products*

For videos that purely focus on the StudEx Meat packaging (like the Wagyu Biltong Gold Reveal), we will use **Higgsfield's Sora 2 Trends** engine.

### The Playbook for Biltong:
*   **The Input:** Take one hyper-clean, high-resolution photo of the Black & Gold StudEx Biltong packaging.
*   **The Prompt Rule:** Keep it under 25 words. Focus on mood and motion.
    *   *Example Prompt:* "Macro shot, slow pan over Black and Gold StudEx Wagyu Biltong packaging, dark moody boardroom background, cinematic lighting."
*   **The Preset:** Select **"Luxury Ad"** or **"Minimalist Corporate"** to align with our "Predator/Clinical Luxury" brand DNA. Avoid "Gen-Z TikTok Edit" for our core product.
*   **The Sound:** Use Sora 2's auto-sync audio features to add heavy, cinematic bass or crisp ASMR crunches to match the visual pacing.

## 3. Cinematic B-Roll & Corridors (Russia, Dubai, Rwanda)
*Reference: Higgsfield Cinema Studio*

For the broader world-building shots (e.g., Day 3: Uvelka oat processing facility, Rwandan coffee mountains, Dubai skyline), we use **Cinema Studio**.

### The Prompting Formula:
*   **Formula:** `[Composition] + [Subject] + [Camera Move] + [Mood/Lighting]`
*   **Example 1 (Rwanda Coffee):** "Wide aerial shot. Misty mountains of Karongi District at dawn with coffee terraces. Slow drone push forward. Soft morning lighting, cinematic."
*   **Example 2 (Dubai Hub):** "Low angle shot. Dubai skyline at night with glowing lights. Fast hyperlapse pan. High contrast, neon and gold."
*   **Best Practice:** Keep motion simple. "One clear camera move usually looks more cinematic than stacked moves."

2. **Determine the Goal:**

    *   *Is it a Cinematic Atmosphere shot?* -> Push to **Cinema Studio**.
3. **Lock Start/End Frames:** Use Higgsfield's Start/End frame feature to ensure the video loops perfectly for TikTok and Instagram Reels.
4. **Post:** Upload to social platforms with the exact captions generated from our previous workflow.
